Hi!

My name is Thomas Lucas, and I'm a passionate computer science student who loves to learn and apply what I discover. I'm constantly looking for new concepts to explore and new ways to grow. What excites me most about programming is its power to solve real-world problems that impact people’s lives. My goal is to use technology to make a meaningful difference in people's lives, directly or indirectly.

Over the last year, I’ve led technical teams in academic and personal projects, served as a tutor for computer science students, and acted as president of the Tech Ready society at BYU-Idaho. These roles have helped me grow in both leadership and technical skill. I'm especially drawn to the infrastructure behind systems, algorithm design, and how AI can be used to improve lives. Whether I’m mentoring a peer or tackling a tough coding challenge, I thrive in environments where I can both teach and learn.
